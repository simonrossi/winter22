First Musket


Our first musket

Here is the video of the musket that we have made. We plan on building more and more devices and weapons until we have perfected the art of building them

This musket, and the revolver are flintlock. This means to fire, there is gun powder in the barrel, then an ember is lowered in through a hole on the top of the barrel. You can see this in the blueprints above.

In the video to the left, you can see me pull the trigger that lowers the embers through the hole into the barrel. Then you can see some smoke blow up out of the hole on top of the barrel.

This weapon is powerful, it is capable of blowing a can clean in half in one shot. For safety the end of the barreling near the hole must be flattened very thoroughly as shown in the blueprints above, so that the force goes out the barrel instead of towards you.
